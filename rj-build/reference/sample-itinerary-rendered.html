Day One Thursday, April 23 Group Arrival & Aloha Vibes
Touch down in paradise and breathe in that warm Hawaiian air — you've earned this!

7:00 PM — Meet & Greet at Tiki's Bar · Twin Fin Hotel
Let the island magic begin! Join the crew for our welcome kick-off — your first drink and a spread of delicious appetizers are completely on us. Come ready to hug, laugh, and toast to the birthday queen!
Day Two Friday, April 24 The Birthday Diva's Big Day
Today is ALL about Cheryl! Rise and shine, birthday queen — your kingdom awaits.

Daytime: Join us for a glorious day of sun, surf, and sand at Ala Moana Beach Park — one of Oahu's most beautiful stretches of shoreline. Splash, sunbathe, build memories, and snap a million photos!

6:00 PM — Sunset Toast at Moana Surfrider Hotel · Beach Bar
Raise your glass at one of Hawaii's most iconic and breathtaking beachfront venues. Let's toast, cheer, and celebrate 70 fabulous years of Cheryl!

Attire: Wear your most vibrant tropical colors — think bold florals, bright pinks, turquoise, coral, and sunshine yellow. Let's paint the beach bar beautiful!
Day Three Saturday, April 25 Your Day, Your Way
This is YOUR vacation — vacation your way! Today is completely open for you to fill however your heart desires. Browse the optional excursions below, mix and match, or do absolutely nothing at all — no judgment here.

Optional Excursions — Book What Speaks to You:

Dole Plantation — Wander the world-famous pineapple plantation, get lost in the giant maze, and treat yourself to the legendary Dole Whip.

Diamond Head Hike — Lace up and trek to the summit of Oahu's iconic volcanic crater for sweeping panoramic views of the coastline.

Circle Island Tour with Snorkeling — Cruise the full coastline of Oahu, stop at breathtaking lookouts along the North Shore, and dip into crystal-clear waters for a snorkeling adventure with tropical sea life.

Surf Lessons — Hang ten! First-timers and seasoned wave riders alike will love getting out on the water with a local instructor.

Stand Up Paddleboarding — Glide across calm Hawaiian waters and take in the island scenery from a whole new perspective.

Simply Chill — The beach, a book, a cold drink, and zero obligations. Sometimes the best excursion is no excursion at all.

Day Four Sunday, April 26 Royal Luau
A Hawaiian celebration is never complete without a luau — and this one is fit for a queen!

5:00 PM — Queen's Luau · International Market Place
An unforgettable afternoon of traditional Hawaiian hula, live music, fire dancers, and a magnificent feast celebrating the spirit of the islands. Let's close out this trip in royal style!

Attire: Island look — flowing sundresses, linen shirts, sarongs, and your best island elegance. Think breezy, chic, and island-inspired.
Day Five Monday, April 27 Aloha & Safe Travels
All good things must come to an end — but the memories we made will last forever.

Squeeze in one last sunrise stroll, a fresh poke bowl, or a final coconut on the beach before saying mahalo to this beautiful island. Safe travels everyone — until the next adventure!
